How to Write a News Article

Mastering the art of writing a news article is a key skill in journalism. Follow these steps to learn how to report clearly, fairly, and effectively.

What You'll Need

  • A notepad or digital device for notes
  • Access to a newsworthy event or source
  • Basic understanding of the inverted pyramid structure
  • Word processor or CMS

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Start with a compelling lead that answers who, what, when, where, why, and how.
  2. Follow with the most important facts first — use the inverted pyramid format.
  3. Include direct quotes from people you’ve interviewed to add credibility and perspective.
  4. Use active voice and concise sentences to maintain clarity.
  5. Stick to the facts — keep opinions out unless clearly attributed.
  6. Check for accuracy and verify names, dates, and locations.
  7. Edit for grammar, AP style, and consistency.
