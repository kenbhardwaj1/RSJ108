How to Write a News Article

Assembly time: 15 minutes
Awesome rating: 100% journalist approved

Mastering the art of writing a news article is a key skill in journalism. Follow these steps to learn how to report clearly, fairly, and effectively.

  • Things You'll Need

  • A notepad or digital device for notes
  • Access to a newsworthy event or source
  • Basic understanding of the inverted pyramid structure
  • Word processor or CMS

  1. Start with a compelling lead

    Answer who, what, when, where, why, and how in your first sentence.

  2. Use the inverted pyramid format

    Put the most important details first, and background info later.

  3. Include direct quotes

    Add credibility and insight from real people related to your story.

  4. Use active voice and be concise

    Keep your writing clear and efficient for better readability.

  5. Stick to facts, not opinions

    Unless clearly attributed, your article should remain objective.

  6. Check for accuracy

    Double-check names, dates, locations, and facts before publishing.

  7. Edit your article

    Ensure grammar, spelling, AP style, and clarity are on point.
